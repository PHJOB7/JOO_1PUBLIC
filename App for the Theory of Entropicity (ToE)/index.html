Welcome

The Theory of Entropicity (ToE) is a radical proposal in theoretical physics that redefines entropy — traditionally a statistical measure of disorder — as the fundamental, dynamic field of reality. In this framework, spacetime, gravity, motion and even the speed of light are not primary constants but emergent consequences of entropic flow【423561634356931†L41-L71】. Instead of treating entropy as a passive by‑product, ToE elevates it to the status of a universal field governing all physical processes【525666122072993†L37-L42】.

This app introduces the key concepts, mathematical structures and ongoing research related to the Theory of Entropicity. You can learn about the core principles, explore the Obidi Actions—two variational principles that encode the dynamics of the entropic field—discover articles and preprints, and contribute your own ideas to help extend and refine the theory.

Use the navigation links above to explore. If you are new to the subject, begin with the Core Principles section for an overview. More advanced readers may jump directly to the Obidi Actions page to study the mathematical framework.
